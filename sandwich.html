Sandwich


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • cheese
  • meat
  • lettuce

Instructions

  1. Place 2 slices of bread on plate
  2. Cut slice on cheese and place on bread
  3. Place one leaf of leetuce on top of cheese
  4. Cut a slice of meat to desired thickness and place on lettuce
  5. Place second slice of bread on top and enjoy!
